# blurry-greek-letters
Repository of 1000 blurry greek letter images for transcription tasks. Great example of "unpleasant tasks" for participants to do in a research study.
Original images provided by Ned Augenblick in files from research study: An Experiment on Time Preference and Misprediction in Unpleasant Tasks, Review of Economic Studies, May 2019, with Matthew Rabin
All of the information uploaded here may be found at: https://faculty.haas.berkeley.edu/ned/. Look for the research study cited above and download the zip file containing the pictures, answer keys, and other instructions.
